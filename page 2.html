    Noli Me Tangere

      Maria Clara

    • Maria Clara is known to be the significant other of Crisostomo Ibarra.

    • She's considered as one of the most beautiful girls in town.

    • She studies in Beaterio and stereotyped as meek and religious all the time despite that she has the desire to speak and stand up for herself, she also wants to love with freedom and for her choice not to be made and dictated by someone else.

      Crisostomo Ibarra

    • Crisostomo Ibarra is the son of Don Rafael

    • He is an educated man and spent his time studying in Europe which caused his blindness to the situation of their society as well as the real story of his father's death.

    • His significant other is Maria Clara whom he left for almost 7 years.

      Kapitan Tiyago

    • Kapitan Tiyago is the father of Maria Clara.

    • His wife died due to complications and ilness during the birth of their daughter.

    • Kapitan Tiyago isn't really religious but he just has a lot of statues in his house and somehow it's just for "show" for him to be called religious and for him to be respected.
    • He considers himself as a spaniard even though he has a Filipino blood.
